Perfectly Nineties.

(A nostalgic lover of Serifs).


It might be difficult to define your identity as a creator and to curate an experience for others to understand you and your work. Your image is subjective to individuals who contact with it, regardless of the design. In light of this, I created an experience that not only displayed my work but also my diverse range of creative interests and the convergence of my processes as an artist, designer, and researcher. In order to help visitors understand who I am as a complicated human being and as an autonomous artist, I want to lead them through the deep depths of personal introspection.
